If you click the button at the top of the page you will be asked a few questions about your yard like:

  • Zipcode
  • Lighting
  • Time

After you have answered those three questions LeafLit will generate a list of plants you can grow in your own yard!

Why Gardening is Great!


Not only is gardening a great way to grow some of your own food, it also attracts pollinators and can be quite theraputic. There is nothing more satisfying than seeing the months of your hard work pay off with some delicious food on the table. As long as you aren't growing invasive species your garden will also help the local ecosystem by attracting all sorts of pollinators, birds, and even some creepy crawlies. Studies in the United States and abroad have found that gardening improves your mood and increases your self-esteem. When people spend time in a garden, their anxiety levels drop and they feel less depressed.

Gardening invites you to get outside, interact with other gardeners, and take charge of your own need for exercise, healthy food, and beautiful surroundings.
